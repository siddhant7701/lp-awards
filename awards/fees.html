Frequently Asked Questions

Is GST included in the prices?

No, GST is applicable as per government regulations and will be added at checkout.

Can I get a refund if I do not pass the assessment?

Assessment fees are non-refundable. However, you can attempt again with available retakes.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, special discounts are available for organizations applying for multiple certifications. Contact us for details.

Can I pay in installments?

For organizational awards, installment payment plans are available. Contact us to discuss options.

When will my certificate be issued?

Certificates are issued within 10 business days of successful completion of all assessment steps.
