AWARD CATEGORY

LP Awards

Leadership and Performance awards recognize organizations that demonstrate exceptional commitment to worker safety, implement best practices, and create a strong safety culture.

What is LP Award?

The LP Award is a national-level certification for organizations that have demonstrated leadership in worker safety practices. It recognizes companies that go beyond compliance to build a comprehensive safety culture.

Organization-Level Certification

Validates organizational safety systems and practices

Sector-Specific Assessment

Customized evaluation for your industry

National Recognition

Prestigious certification valid for 3 years

Marketing & Credibility

Use certification in marketing and recruitment

Eligibility Criteria

  • •Minimum 2 years of operational history
  • •Documented safety policies and procedures
  • •Active safety committee or designated officer
  • •No major safety violations in past 12 months
  • •Willingness to undergo assessment

Benefits of LP Certification

Enhanced Credibility

Boost your organization's reputation as a safety leader

Competitive Advantage

Stand out in bids and contracts with recognized certification

Reduced Incidents

Lower workplace accidents and associated costs

Employee Retention

Attract and retain talented workers seeking safe workplaces

Insurance Benefits

Potential insurance premium reductions

Regulatory Compliance

Meet and exceed government safety standards

Assessment Process

1

Online Assessment

Complete a comprehensive online assessment about your organization's safety practices, policies, and procedures.

2

Document Review

Submit safety documentation, incident reports, training records, and certifications for expert review.

3

Physical Inspection

On-site visit by certified assessors to verify practices, inspect facilities, and interview personnel.

4

Certification

Upon successful completion, receive your LP Award certification valid for 3 years.
